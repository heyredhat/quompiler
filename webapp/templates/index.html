QUOMPILER!

THE GOAL:

  • Try to move the white stars onto the red stars.
  • You control the colorful inner stars.

CONTROLS:

  • LEFT JOYSTICK: rotate camera
  • RIGHT JOYSTICK: rotate selected inner star

  • LEFT/RIGHT D-PAD: select inner star

  • LEFT BUMPER: view from outside
  • RIGHT BUMPER: view from selected inner star

  • A: select another inner star to tangle with
  • LEFT TRIGGER: untangle
  • RIGHT TRIGGER: tangle

  • HOME: toggle this help window
  • BACK: reset game
  • START: new game
  • Y: fewer qubits
  • B: more qubits

  • NOTE: Works with XBOX 360 controller! Install driver, for Mac, here.

WHY?

  • You are compiling down a complicated quantum computer program, which is a certain unitary operator acting on many qubits at once, into a series of local, elementary operations: rotations of single qubits, and also an entangling operation (CNOT) on only two qubits at a time.
  • HINT: The opacity of the blue sphere ranges from p = 0 to 1, where p is the probability of hopping from the current state to the desired state. It is given by the square of the inner product between the two state vectors. When p = 0, the two states are orthogonal aka at right angles, and the sphere is transparent; when p is close enough to 1, you win!
